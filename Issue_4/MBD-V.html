Olanzapine orally disintegrating tablets: A better alternative to standard tablets
The PLATYPUS Study
by Medeka Health | February 01, 2014
Corcq et al: A study in adolescent patients

Corzq et al compared the effects of 12-week monotherapy with olanzapine orally disintegrating tablets (n=16) and olanzapine standard tablets (n=10) on weight and body mass index (BMI). They observed that adolescents receiving olanzapine orally disintegrating tablets had lesser weight gain compared to olanzapine standard tablets (Figure 3A & B).6

Significant weight loss after switching to orally disintegrating tablets

A long-term, open-label, prospective study by Chawla et al reported that switching schizophrenic patients (n=26) from olanzapine standard tablets to olanzapine orally disintegrating tablets offered significant weight loss. At the end of follow-up:7

  • Average weight loss was 2.7±0.7 kg (p=0.001)
  • About 82% of patients reported weight loss, while 18% of patients exhibited no weight change or weight gain Mean reduction in BMI was 1.0±0.3 kg/m2 (p=0.001) Weight-loss was maintained over 12 months.
