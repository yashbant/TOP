Olanzapine orally disintegrating tablets: A better alternative to standard tablets
The PLATYPUS Study
by Medeka Health | February 01, 2014
Proposed mechanism of lesser weight gain

Majority of atypical antipsychotic drugs induce weight gain as a result of appetite stimulation mediated by their effects on serotonin receptors. Olanzapine oral disintegrating tablets, owing to faster dissolution profile (< 1 minute), may bypass gastrointestinal metabolism before making contact with serotonin receptors, hence causing lesser weight gain.3,8
